Welcome to Autopilot!

Thank you for downloading our extension! We're here to help you maximize your Aeroplan rewards. To start, open the Air Canada website, select "Book with points".

Book with Aeroplan points

Enter your flight information and search for flights. As soon as you reach the flight results page, Autopilot calculates the cost per point (CPP) for each flight automatically!

Please note that Autopilot only supports redemptions made on Air Canada, and plans to support more airlines in the future.

Badges

Aeroplan points are currently valued at an average of 2.1 CPP (CAD).* The higher the CPP of a given flight, the more you save by using points!

3.0 CPP

Flights valued over 2.1 CPP. It's smart to pay with points for these flights.

5.0 CPP

The ultimate deal. Flights with this badge offer the highest value for your points.

1.0 CPP

Flights valued lower than 2.1 CPP. For these flights, it's better to pay with cash.

For more information, hover your mouse over any badge. A small window will pop up showing how much you could save by choosing to pay with points or cash.

If no badge appears on a flight, it means a matching cash fare could not be found for that route.

*2.1 cents/point is the average valuation of Aeroplan points as recommended by Prince of Travel as of April 2024.
