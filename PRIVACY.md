# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 17, 2026*

## Overview

Autopilot is a Chrome extension that calculates the cents-per-point (CPP) value of Aeroplan point redemptions on Air Canada flight search results. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles data.

## Data Collection

Autopilot does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The extension does not require users to create an account or provide any personal information.

## What the Extension Accesses

Autopilot reads flight fare information (point prices, cash prices, flight times, and cabin classes) displayed on Air Canada's website. This data is processed entirely within your browser to calculate CPP values. No fare data is sent to any external server.

## Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's built-in session storage to temporarily hold flight data for the current browsing session. This data is automatically deleted when the browser is closed. The extension also stores a simple flag to track whether the welcome screen has been shown. No personal information is stored at any time.

## Third-Party Services

Autopilot does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, advertising, or data collection services.

## Permissions

The extension requests browser permissions solely to function on Air Canada's website. These permissions are used to read fare data, detect page navigation, and display CPP badges. No permissions are used to monitor browsing activity, collect personal data, or access any websites other than those required for fare comparison.

## Feedback Form

If you choose to submit feedback through our Google Form, any information you provide (such as your email address or responses) is collected solely to improve Autopilot. Submitting feedback is entirely voluntary. Feedback responses are handled through Google Forms and are governed by Google's privacy policy.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted with an updated date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out through the feedback form linked in the extension, or open an issue on GitHub.
